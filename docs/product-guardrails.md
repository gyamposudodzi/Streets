# Product Guardrails

## Positioning

Streets is a creator booking platform. Product language should stay centered on creators, bookings, fulfillment, moderation, trust, and platform safety.

## Hard Rules

- No explicit sexual-service wording in listings, categories, flows, or marketing copy
- No prostitution or escorting workflows
- No off-platform payment bait
- No public disclosure of exact personal location
- No public disclosure of direct personal contact details

## In-Person Rules

- In-person bookings may be supported through neutral logistics workflows
- Exact meeting details should only appear after paid and accepted bookings
- Sensitive location and coordination changes must be logged
- In-person offerings require stronger trust and moderation controls

## Moderation Rules

Flag or reject content that:

- solicits unlawful activity
- uses explicit sexual-service language
- attempts to move users off platform before booking
- includes direct personal contact details in public content
- attempts to bypass payments, age gates, or moderation
